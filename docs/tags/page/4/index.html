published on April 27, 2021
NTCP 2 Category: Transports Last updated: 2021-03 Accurate for: 0.9.50 Overview NTCP2 is an authenticated key agreement protocol that improves the resistance of [NTCP] to various forms of automated identification and attacks. NTCP2 is designed for flexibility and coexistence with NTCP. It may be supported on the same port as NTCP, or a different port, or without simultaneous NTCP support at all.
